Hi, I’m Norlyn Piquero, a Chemical Engineer turned Freelancer with a passion for the digital world. Since 2018, I’ve been helping businesses and individuals create functional, responsive websites, starting with hand-coding using HTML, CSS, and a bit of jQuery.
